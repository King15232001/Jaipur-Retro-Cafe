About Jaipur Retro Cafe

A vibrant cafe in Jawahar Nagar for food, comfort and conversations.

Jaipur Retro Cafe is located at 7-TA-20, Sector 7, Jawahar Nagar, Jaipur. Known for its rooftop ambience, indoor and outdoor seating, pocket-friendly food and youthful vibe, it is a popular choice for casual dining, hangouts, dates, lunch and dinner.

✓Rooftop, indoor and outdoor seating available.
✓Popular for fast food, Indian food, beverages, sandwiches, snacks and group ordering.
✓Dine-in, drive-through, takeaway and no-contact delivery options.
✓Suitable for couples, friends, students, birthdays and casual group plans.
